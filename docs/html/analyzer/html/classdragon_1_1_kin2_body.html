Default ctor for radiative capture.

Class dragon::Kin2Body //////////////////////////////// Class for relativistic 2 body reaction kinematics

Calculates relativistic kinematic relationships for 2 body reactions. Consider the reaction

where, by convention, the projectile with mass and kinetic energy and is incident on a (stationary) target of mass . The kinematics of this reaction can be described by its (invariant) squared 4-momentum

Default ctor for radiative capture Sets beam and target masses from AME12 compilation. Energies are initially unset, use SetE*() functions to specify an energy.

Parameters
projectilestring specifying projectile nucleus (e.g. - "7Be", "22Ne", etc.)
targetstring specifying projectile nucleus (e.g. - "p", "1H", "4He", "alpha", etc.)
projectilestring specifying ejectile nucleus (e.g. - "p", "d", etc.)
energybeam energy in units corresponding to specified frame string
framestring specifying frame / units of beam energy
qbcharge state of beam (default = 0)
