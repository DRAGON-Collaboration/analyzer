Calculate beam energy from MD1 field.

Energy is calculated using equation 2 from Dave Hutcheon's NIM paper on BGO z-position E/m = cmd1 * (qB/m)<sup>2</sup> - [1/(2*u*c<sup>2</sup>)] * (E/m)<sup>2</sup>
